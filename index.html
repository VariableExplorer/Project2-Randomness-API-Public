The Shifting Ruins

Welcome to the shifting dungeons, adventurer! Are you ready to face the perils that await in hopes of finding glory? Well, then, move around the dungeons with the W, A, S, and D keys. Walk over the green squares to find the loot! Walk over the brown doors to enter new rooms. But beware of the red circles, they are the guardians of the dungeon and will put an end to your adventure if they catch you! Good luck, adventurer!
